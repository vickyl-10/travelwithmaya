I can't believe it's finally happening! After months of dreaming about Costa Rica's lush rainforests, pristine beaches, and incredible biodiversity, I'm officially planning my adventure to this Central American paradise. The "pura vida" lifestyle has been calling my name, and I'm ready to answer!

Why Costa Rica? ü§î

Costa Rica has been on my bucket list for years, and for good reason! This small but mighty country packs an incredible punch when it comes to natural beauty and adventure. From the Pacific to the Caribbean, you can experience everything from cloud forests to tropical beaches in just a few hours' drive.

What really draws me to Costa Rica is its commitment to sustainability and eco-tourism. With over 25% of the country protected as national parks and reserves, it's a paradise for nature lovers like me. Plus, the "pura vida" philosophy of living life to the fullest resonates deeply with my travel philosophy.

Costa Rica's diverse climate means I need to pack smart! From the cool cloud forests to the hot beaches, I'll need layers and the right gear for adventure.
